Recruiter Guidelines

Posting Guidelines

Please follow these rules to ensure a smooth and professional experience for everyone.

Genuine Vacancies Only

Post only real and currently available job openings. No promotional content or false leads. All vacancies are verified before publishing.

Clear Contact Information

Provide accurate phone numbers, WhatsApp numbers, Email so Candidate can forward applicant details to you seamlessly.

Add Extra Requirements

Include optional details like age limits, language preferences, or other instructions in the "Additional Info" section of the form.

Strictly No Scams

Fraudulent posts will result in a permanent ban. We actively monitor and verify postings for community safety.

Post Multiple Vacancies

If the same role has different preferences (e.g., experience levels), submit each variation as a separate vacancy.

confirmation on job post

We will notify you within 12 hours if your vacancy is approved or not. If approved, it will be posted within 24 hours.
