Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how JobInfo collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • WhatsApp phone number
  • Job preferences and location
  • Form submissions (e.g., job applications or vacancy postings)
  • Communication metadata (e.g., timestamps, job codes)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide, improve, and personalize our job alert services. This includes:

  • Sending job alerts via WhatsApp
  • Connecting job seekers and recruiters
  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improving the functionality and relevance of our platform

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your data only when:

  • Required by law or regulatory authorities
  • To facilitate communications with recruiters you interact with
  • To process messages via WhatsApp Business API, provided by Meta Platforms

4. WhatsApp and Meta Integration

JobInfo uses Meta’s WhatsApp Business API for communication. By using our services, you acknowledge that your messages and metadata may be processed by Meta in accordance with Meta’s Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to secure your data using tools provided by Google (e.g., Google Sheets, Drive) and secure server connections. However, no method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access or correct your data
  • Opt out of receiving messages (reply “STOP” on WhatsApp)
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the Support Form or via WhatsApp.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of JobInfo after changes are posted constitutes your agreement to the new terms.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Support Form or WhatsApp.

10. Legal Basis for Data Processing

We process your information based on your consent, our legitimate interests in operating the JobInfo platform, and to fulfill our contractual obligations to you.

11. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

12. Third-party Services

Our platform may integrate with third-party services such as Google Sheets. Please review their respective privacy policies for more details:

  • Google Privacy Policy

13. Cookies and Tracking

We currently do not use cookies or tracking scripts on our platform. If that changes, we will update this section accordingly.
