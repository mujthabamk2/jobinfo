About Us

About

Jobinfo is an innovative solution that enables you to find vacancies easily via WhatsApp.

Say goodbye to endless job searches! Find your dream job at your favourite chat application, unlock your career with a simple message!

We started with a simple goal: to make job searching easier for people who aren’t familiar with job portals or online platforms. Many job seekers in India are comfortable using WhatsApp but find traditional job websites confusing or inaccessible.

Through Jobinfo,

  • Users can receive real-time job updates, request detailed information, and easily apply for positions—all through WhatsApp.
  • Jobinfo connect you with your dream job and simplify the process of bridging the gap between recruiters and job seekers with our simple and mobile-friendly solution.
  • We aim to empower individuals in mid-level and entry-level positions, as well as in white-collar jobs across various fields.
